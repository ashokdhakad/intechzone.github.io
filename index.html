About Intech Zone

IntechZone provides web and mobile software solutions from Startups and SMEs. We offer our services with a range of ready-solutions, latest open-source frameworks and bespoke software development.
We have our development centre in Indore, India, which is the business capital of the state of Madhya Pradesh. Our sales representatives are in UK, USA, Sweden and Australia. We focused on User Experience Design, Web Development and Mobile App Solutions. Intechzone is working with domain such as IOT, Telecom, Hospitality, Logistics and Retail. Our Technical solution range from latest frameworks, development methodologies, open-source architectures to native and hybrid solutions. Focused on Start-ups and entrepreneurs, our On-Demand Services solutions are designed to provide our clients the go-to-market edge in a variety of domains such as health and fitness, beauty and appearance, education, medical and other service based industries.
